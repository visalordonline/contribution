FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Prosperous Financial Services

Account & Registration

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Sign Up" button on our website, fill in your personal details including name, email, phone number, and password. Complete the verification process, and your account will be ready to use.
What information do I need to register?
You'll need to provide your full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, NIN (National Identification Number), marital status, occupation, state, LGA, residential address, and bank details for processing withdrawals.
How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password securely.
Can I update my profile information?
Yes, you can update your email address through your profile. For other details, please contact our support team at support@prosperouscooperative.com as these require admin verification.

Contributions

What is a contribution?
A contribution is a deposit you make into your account with Prosperous Financial Services. Based on your daily contribution amount, you earn "ticks" that track your participation. Each tick represents one day of contribution.
What are the different contribution plans?
We offer two main plans: Daily Contribution Plan (31 days) and 6-Month Contribution Plan (180 days). Choose the plan that suits your financial goals.
How are ticks calculated?
Ticks are calculated by dividing your deposit amount by your daily registration amount. For example, if your daily rate is ₦1,000 and you deposit ₦5,000, you'll get 5 ticks.
Can I add more funds to my contribution?
Yes! You can add income (additional funds) to your existing contribution through the "Add Income" button. This will increase your ticks accordingly.
What happens when I complete all ticks?
When you complete all ticks in your contribution plan, your status will change to "Complete" and you'll be eligible for withdrawal or reinvestment in a new contribution cycle.

Withdrawals & Payments

How do I withdraw my funds?
Click the "Withdraw" button on your contribution card, enter the amount you wish to withdraw, and submit your request. The funds will be transferred to your registered bank account.
How long does withdrawal take?
Withdrawal processing typically takes 3-5 business days, depending on your bank. Some banks may process faster during business hours. You'll receive a confirmation notification once processed.
Are there withdrawal limits?
Withdrawal limits may apply based on your account status and verification level. You cannot withdraw more than your available balance. Contact our support team for details about your specific limits.
Are there fees for withdrawals?
Bank transfer fees may be deducted from your withdrawal amount. The exact fees depend on your bank. We'll show you the deduction before you confirm the withdrawal.
How many times can I withdraw?
You can withdraw up to 4 times per contribution cycle. This is to ensure stability and proper fund management. Additional withdrawals may be subject to special approval.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. Your data is stored securely and never shared with unauthorized parties.

How is my personal information used?
Your information is used to manage your account, process transactions, comply with regulations, and improve our services. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Technical Support

What should I do if I can't log in?
Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" option. If this doesn't work, clear your browser cache or try a different browser. Contact support if the issue persists.
Does the app work on mobile devices?
Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices. You can also save it as a web app for easier access. No separate app download is required.
What browsers are supported?
We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, please use the latest version of your browser.
